Empower Young Adults from IP Communities

Endonela Institute of Technology Foundation, Inc. provides financial assistance to young adults from Indigenous Peoples communities who lack the resources to pursue education or skills training. Your contribution will help them access programs that prepare them for work and empower them to build a better future.

We also welcome general donations, which will go directly to supporting all initiatives of Endonela Institute of Technology Foundation, Inc. aimed at uplifting young adults from underserved communities across the country. Every contribution makes a meaningful impact in helping these individuals achieve their goals.
